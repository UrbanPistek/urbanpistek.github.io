As part of CERN's Beamline for Schools Competition myself and the other Co-Leads founded a Team - Investigate Tachyons - to enter the competition. As part of the competition teams submitted a proposal outlining a physics experiment that could be performed on a beamline at CERN. The proposal put forward was a Physics theory outlining the possible existence of imaginary mass based on how force acts the same on all objects. The team was featured on a news segment on CTV, was invited to the Alberta Legislature to speak with the current minister of education and I gave a keynote talk about the project at Telus Spark Science Center.
