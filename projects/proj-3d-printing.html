3D Printer Modifications

Project information

  • Type: Mechanical
  • Skills: 3D Printing | CAD
  • Role: Personal Project
  • Background: Developing modifications to enhance my 3D printer

While still a work in progress, this project is an on-going effort to enhance and customize my 3D printer into what I desire. Currently some custom parts have been made to make small adjustments and modifications while connecting and setting up OctoPi on a raspberry pi 4 to enable remote acces, control and monitoring. The next major modifcation is to modify the power supply to have a dedicated 5V rail from it to power the raspberry pi along with additional electronics to be added running on 5V.
